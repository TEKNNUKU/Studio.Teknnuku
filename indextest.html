TEKNNUKU STUDIO · Contractor Agreement
Probationary Contractor Agreement
⚠️ Please Read Carefully
This agreement governs your engagement with TEKNNUKU Studio during and after the probationary period. By signing, you confirm you have read, understood, and accept all terms below.
1. Core Philosophy

TEKNNUKU Studio operates on a Performance Before Pay Stability model. Entry requires proving value. Growth earns stability. Rewards are tied exclusively to results. TEKNNUKU does not "hire people" — TEKNNUKU activates producers.

2. Workforce Classification
Stage 1 · Days 0–30 · Probationary Contractor
Independent contractor status. Performance-based engagement only. No guaranteed salary. Full access to tools. Daily reporting required.
Stage 2 · Days 30–60 · Provisional Employee
Semi-integrated team member. Base salary begins. Performance still monitored. KPI ≥ 80% required to proceed to confirmation.
Stage 3 · 60+ Days · Confirmed Employee
Full team member. Full compensation package unlocked. Performance bonuses active.
3. Probation Phases & Requirements
Phase 1 · Activation (Days 1–14)
No base salary. Commission or task-based pay only. Minimum one measurable output required. No output = immediate exit without further notice.
Phase 2 · Proof (Days 15–30)
Consistency required. Optional micro-stipend may begin at discretion of management. KPI ≥ 60–70% of target required. Underperformance results in termination or role downgrade.
Phase 3 · Transition (Days 30–60)
Base salary introduced. Full responsibilities. ≥ 80% performance confirms employment. 60–79% extends probation. Below 60% results in exit.
4. Payment Terms
  • →No guaranteed salary during Stage 1 (Days 0–30)
  • →Commission is only paid on revenue actually received by TEKNNUKU
  • →Task/milestone pay is only released upon verified completion
  • →All pay structures are set out in your role-specific KPI & Compensation Sheet
  • →Bonus pools are distributed based on verified individual contribution
5. Performance Evaluation
Performance is evaluated on a weighted model: Output (50%) — revenue generated, tasks delivered, features shipped; Consistency (30%) — daily execution and reporting; Behaviour (20%) — ownership, communication, and professionalism.
6. Termination Conditions
TEKNNUKU Studio reserves the right to terminate engagement immediately, without prior notice, for any of the following:
  • ✗No activity or output for 2+ consecutive days
  • ✗Failure to meet Phase 1 minimum output requirement
  • ✗Missed daily reporting without prior notice
  • ✗Poor conduct, dishonesty, or disrespectful behaviour
  • ✗Performance below 60% threshold in any evaluation phase
7. Required Documentation
Upon engagement, you will be required to sign: (1) Probationary Contractor Agreement; (2) KPI & Compensation Sheet; (3) Role SOP Playbook; (4) Payment Terms Agreement. This application submission constitutes your initial agreement to these terms. Full documentation will be provided upon shortlisting.
8. Company Performance Bonus
When company monthly revenue reaches ₦5M+, a team bonus pool is activated. When it reaches ₦10M+, a larger pool is distributed. Allocation is based on verified individual contribution across the evaluation period.
